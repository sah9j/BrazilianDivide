Goal for Website

The site addresses the United Nations’ goal number 10, which aims to reduce inequalities both within a country and how countries interact with each other. Brazil faces many such inequalities within its borders, ranging from societal differences based on gender, economic status, education, and skin color. Each of the UN’s targets and indicators can be directly related to these problems; in addition to pages that give an overview of the country, other pages will tackle these inequalities and tie them to the goals. Through this project, our group has learned a lot about the country, its culture, and the people who inhabit it.
